# Project Purpose

## What is City of Seekers?

City of Seekers (COS) is a transformational festival taking place **June 11-14, 2026** in **Hameln, Germany**. It's a space for people to make new connections and deepen existing ones in an open, fun setting.

## Website Goals

This website serves as the official hub for:

1. **Festival Information** - What COS is about, dates, location
2. **Ticketing** - Purchase tickets (currently disabled, coming soon)
3. **Team Profiles** - Meet the organizing team
4. **FAQs** - Answer common questions
5. **Community Engagement** - WhatsApp group links, Instagram
6. **Legal Compliance** - Imprint, privacy policy, code of conduct

## Target Audience

- Potential festival attendees
- Community members from the YesFam/Yes Theory community
- People seeking transformational experiences
- International audience (hence EN/DE language support)

## Brand Identity

- **Slogan:** "Come as a stranger, leave as a friend!"
- **Colors:** Warm orange palette (`cos-main-orange`), accent colors
- **Tone:** Welcoming, adventurous, community-focused
- **Font:** Omnes (custom)

## Key Features

- Mobile-responsive design
- Bilingual (English/German)
- Video background on hero section
- Photo gallery showcasing past events
- Google Maps integration (with cookie consent)
- Countdown timer to event
